About the P&C

Your voice in the school community

The KHS P&C is a volunteer-led not-for-profit that works directly with the principal and staff to strengthen our school for every family.

1
School Partnership
We engage directly with school leadership on everything from student wellbeing to capital programs and policy. Your concerns are heard at the highest level.
2
Uniform Shop Operations
We operate the KHS Uniform Shop. All proceeds flow directly back into funding school programs — not profit. Open Tuesdays and Fridays, 11am–2pm during term.
3
Fundraising & Events
We run events and fundraisers that bring the school community together and generate tangible resources for students.
4
Open to Everyone
All parents and carers are welcome to attend meetings and get involved. No commitment required — simply showing up makes a difference.

Why the P&C matters

At KHS, the P&C has a close working relationship with the school. Parents are committed to ensuring that the school maintains high-quality facilities and programs for all 1,649 students.

What We Do

Our key focus areas

Six ways the P&C makes a direct, measurable difference to every student and family at Killara High School.

📚
Learning Support
We fund resources, programs and initiatives that enhance classroom learning and give our outstanding teaching staff the tools they need to excel.
💬
Parent Voice
Monthly P&C meetings give every parent a direct seat at the table. Your ideas and concerns reach school leadership — and are acted on.
👕
Uniform Shop
Volunteer-operated, community-focused. All proceeds fund school programs. Endorsed by the school community through the P&C. Open Tues & Fri 11am–2pm.
🧠
Student Wellbeing
We advocate for and fund mental health resources, pastoral care programs, and social initiatives that support students beyond the classroom.
🎪
Community Events
From welcome nights for Year 7 families to end-of-year celebrations — we build occasions that bring the entire KHS community together.
💰
Fundraising
Strategic fundraising campaigns generating real funding for equipment, excursions, school infrastructure and enrichment programs that benefit all students.
